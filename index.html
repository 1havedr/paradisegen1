FAQ

Common Questions

How fast will I receive my account?
Delivery is instant — credentials are sent to your email address automatically within seconds of your payment being confirmed by Stripe.
Is my payment secure?
Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified. We never store your card information.
What's the difference between the two account types?
Unbanned accounts have a clean record and full access to Hypixel. Banned accounts are restricted from Hypixel specifically but work on all other Minecraft servers — perfect for alts.

Join our Discord server for live support. Our team is active daily and will help resolve any issues quickly.
