FAQ
How fast is delivery?
Delivery is fully automated. Once payment is confirmed, you'll receive your account credentials via email within seconds.
What payment methods are accepted?
We use Stripe for secure card processing. All major credit and debit cards are accepted.
What happens if I don't receive my account?
Check your spam folder first. If you still haven't received anything, reach out on our Discord and we'll resolve it immediately.
Are accounts guaranteed to work?
Yes — all accounts are verified before being added to stock. If you experience any issues, contact us on Discord for a replacement.
Do you offer bulk orders?
Yes! For bulk orders of 10+ accounts, message us on Discord for a custom deal and pricing.
